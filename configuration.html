How the Card Works and How You Manage It

The Card Experience

Tap your Residue card on a compatible phone and your digital profile opens instantly. It brings your identity, contact details, and key links together in one polished view that feels clear, premium, and easy to follow.

Your profile can also include a company section, giving space for your business name, logo, and supporting details alongside your personal information. That makes the card useful for both personal introductions and brand-led interactions.

Visitors can save your contact details, move to the right link quickly, and understand who you are without digging through clutter. The result is a smooth handoff that feels intentional from the first tap.

Card Management

Once you sign in to the Residue dashboard, you can shape exactly what people see when your card is tapped. Personal details, company information, and links can all be updated from one controlled space.

Each section is managed individually, so it stays simple to refine your profile without rebuilding everything. You can keep information current, adjust what matters most, and maintain a presentation that fits your brand.

Display toggles let you hide or reveal sections whenever you need, while your saved content stays ready in the background. Light and dark modes help you keep the final profile clean, refined, and consistent.
