About

Quiet rebellion. Controlled presence.

We believe there is a different kind of confidence.


Not loud.

Not performative.

Not constantly asking to be seen.


The kind that walks into a room and doesn’t need to explain itself.

If you’ve ever felt slightly out of place in overly polished networking rooms…

If you’ve ever preferred depth over display…

If you value precision, restraint, and quiet intent…


You already understand why Residue exists.

Residue was built from a simple observation:

Most identity today is transactional.


Profiles. Links. Metrics. Visibility.

We wanted something physical.


Something deliberate.

Something that doesn’t chase attention, but commands it.

Residue is for people who move with intention.

Who care about material, not noise.

Who believe that what remains after the introduction matters more than the introduction itself.


It is not a mass product.

It is a signal.


A quiet layer of identity for individuals who choose control over performance.

If that resonates —

you’re already part of it.

Privacy and data: Contact, access, and purchase information may be processed for account management, fulfillment, fraud prevention, and support, in line with applicable privacy law.
By using this platform, you consent to the collection and use of your information for operational purposes. Residue implements reasonable safeguards to protect your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted online.

Payments and invoicing: Orders are subject to payment authorization and stock availability. Invoices, payment status, and order events may be recorded in our secure systems for operational and compliance purposes.
All payments are processed through secure third-party providers. Residue does not store full card details and relies on trusted payment gateways for transaction processing.

Order processing and delivery: Orders are processed within 1–2 business days from confirmed payment. Customisation requests, including logo engraving or personalised details, are completed during this processing period.
Delivery timelines depend on the selected shipping method and destination within South Africa. Standard delivery typically occurs within 2–5 business days after dispatch. Customers will be notified once their order has been shipped.
Each order includes a Residue NFC card and protective packaging. Delivery timelines are estimates and may vary due to courier operations or external factors beyond our control.
Please note that Residue currently operates exclusively within South Africa. Orders are only accepted from customers and businesses based in South Africa, and delivery is limited to addresses within the country. We do not offer international shipping at this time, and our services are intended solely for use within the South African market.

Returns and refunds: Due to the custom nature of our product, we do not accept returns or offer refunds. If you have any issues with your order, please contact our support team for assistance.
If a product arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, customers must contact us within 7 days of delivery. Verified issues may qualify for a replacement or appropriate resolution at Residue’s discretion.
Refunds, where applicable, will be processed through the original payment method.

Digital service and access Each purchase includes access to a personalised digital profile page linked to the NFC card. This page is accessible when the card is tapped on a compatible device and can be updated by the user.
